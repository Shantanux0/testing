0 Executive Summary

4 The "Swapping AI": Mathematical Deep Dive

This is the "Heart" of the platform. It is a Heuristic Expert System built in Java. It doesn't just match keywords; it matches human compatibility.

1. The Weighted Sum Model (The Math)

Our AI takes raw data and normalizes it to a 0-1 scale. Then, it applies the following priority matrix:

Final_Score = (Test × 0.30) + (LevelGap × 0.20) + (Goals × 0.10) + (Time × 0.10) + (Certs × 0.10) + (Reputation × 0.10) + (Style × 0.05) + (Commitment × 0.05)

2. Heuristic Logic Breakdown (The Logic)

Proficiency Gap Logic

Our AI uses a 'Peer-to-Mentor' bias. If a teacher is 1 level above you, the score is 1.0. If they are 4 levels above, the score drops to 0.8 because experts can be too advanced for beginners. If they are below you, the score drops to 0.4.

Goal Compatibility Logic

The AI matches 'Motivations'. A learner who wants 'Job Prep' is a perfect match (1.0) for a teacher who 'Loves Teaching', but a poor match (0.6) for someone just looking to 'Network'.

5 Two-Tier AI Strategy

To be technically accurate, our platform uses two distinct types of AI:

Tier 1: External LLM (The Brain)

Uses Gemini/OpenAI for text tasks. It handles Question Generation, distractor creation, and Sentiment Analysis of feedback.

Tier 2: Internal Swapping AI (The Matchmaker)

This is our proprietary logic. It uses the WSM algorithm and a Learning Loop that learns your preferences based on which teachers you interact with most.

7 Expert Q&A

Q: Is this a Neural Network?

A: No, this is a Heuristic Expert System. While neural networks are black boxes, our system is transparent and controllable, ensuring every match can be explained to the user.

Q: How does the AI learn?

A: Our internal Swapping AI uses a Feedback Loop. Every time a session is completed, the AI updates the user's reputation and goal-affinity scores, refining future matches automatically.
